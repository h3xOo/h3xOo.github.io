Bogo Indian Defense
===================

# Brief summary


The Bogo-Indian Defence is a chess opening characterised by the moves: 1. d4 Nf6. 2. c4 e6. 3. Nf3 Bb4+. The position after 1.d4 Nf6 2.c4 e6 is common.
