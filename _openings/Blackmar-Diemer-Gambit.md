Blackmar-Diemer Gambit
======================

# Brief summary


The Blackmar-Diemer Gambit (or BDG) is a chess opening characterized by the moves: . 1. d4 d5 2. e4 dxe4 3. Nc3. where White intends to follow up with f3, usually on the fourth move.
